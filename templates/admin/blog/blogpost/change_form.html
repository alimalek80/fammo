📸 Adding Alt Text to Images

Thumbnail Image: Use the "Image alt" field above to describe your thumbnail image.

Content Images (Markdown): When adding images in the content field, include descriptive alt text using this syntax:

![descriptive alt text here](image-url)

Example: ![A brown Labrador dog playing fetch in a sunny park](https://example.com/dog.jpg)

Why it matters: Alt text improves accessibility for screen readers and helps with SEO.
