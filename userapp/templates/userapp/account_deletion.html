Account Deletion Policy

App Name: Fammo
Developer: Fammo AI
Last Updated: December 16, 2025

How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Fammo account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: support@fammo.ai
  2. Subject line: "Delete My Account"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your registered email address
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  4. Wait for confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your request is processed
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before submitting your request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your Fammo account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User Account Information: Email address, password, and authentication tokens
  • Profile Data: Name, phone number, address, and location preferences
  • Pet Profiles: All pet information including names, breeds, health data, and dietary preferences
  • AI Recommendations: All AI-generated meal plans and health reports for your pets
  • Uploaded Media: Pet photos, profile images, and any other uploaded files
  • Clinic Information (if applicable): Clinic details, logos, and verification documents
  • Chat History: All conversations with veterinarians
  • Subscription Information: Subscription preferences and usage history

What Data May Be Retained

For legal, compliance, and security purposes, some data may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form:

  • Transaction Records: Payment and billing records required by law for financial compliance (retained for 7 years as per legal requirements)
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics for platform improvement (retained for 2 years)
  • Legal Records: Records required for dispute resolution or legal proceedings (retained as legally required)
  • Backup Systems: Data in automatic backups will be deleted within 90 days as part of our backup rotation cycle
All retained data will be anonymized and cannot be linked back to your identity.

Deletion Timeframe

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your deletion request.

You will receive an email confirmation once the deletion process is complete.
